About Us

Dec online shop is an online store that provides a complete list of products from the Dec store. You can place orders and receive goods without standing in line. Our online store was created to reduce queues and increase the availability of goods for campers.

Creators

Programmers: Pasha Druzhuk, Vanya Solomatin, Gleb Geo

Delivery

Delivery takes place only during quiet hours and phone time.

You can order no more than 3 meals per day.

Our store provides 2 types of delivery:
∘ Express (89 Dec.), provides you with delivery from 21.15-21.35
∘ Standard (21 Dec.), provides you with next day delivery in a quiet hour.

If the customer does not have Decs, delivery will be postponed the next day with an additional payment in 5 Decs.
